📖 About GraceVoice

GraceVoice is your voice-powered Bible companion, built to help you listen, follow, and reflect on scripture hands-free.

Whether you're an elderly reader, someone with limited eyesight, or just prefer hearing the Word, GraceVoice makes it easy to:

  • 🔊 Listen to chapters and verses read aloud
  • 🎙️ Use simple voice commands like "Read John 3 verse 16"
  • 💾 Bookmark favorite verses and notes
  • 📱 Install and use offline on any device

Example: Say “Read Matthew chapter 5” after opening GraceVoice, and it will begin reading.

This app works best when already open. Voice assistants like Siri or Google Assistant may support shortcuts in future updates.

GraceVoice does not require internet to read previously opened chapters. For full offline use, we are working on complete Bible loading.
